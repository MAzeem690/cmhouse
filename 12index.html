Education

Punjab is home to 60% of Pakistan’s citizens most of whom are young, the goal is to provide them with a robust social, academic, and global education that will ensure a prosperous future.

Education is the process of acquiring knowledge, skills, values, and habits through teaching, learning, or self-study. It starts at a young age and continues throughout life in both formal (school, college) and informal (life experience, training) settings.
